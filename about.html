About the Chef

Food is home, and that's where the heart is.

Hi, I'm Chef Kaela and welcome to our virtual kitchen! I'm a self-taught chef who loves to cook, bake and eat. I've been cooking for as long as I can remember and it's something that's been instilled in me from my family. I grew up in a household where food was the center of everything; it was a way to bring people together, to celebrate, to comfort, and to show love. I've always been passionate about food and the joy it brings to people. There's nothing like being able to cook not only for yourself, but for your loved ones as well. I've learned a lot along the way, and I'm here to share my passion for food and crafting drinks with you as well as help you create delicious meals in your home. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced cook, I hope you'll find something here that inspires you to get creative in the kitchen.

"Cooking is like love. It should be entered into with abandon or not at all." - Harriet Van Horne
